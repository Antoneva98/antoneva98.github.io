About

Catch what one metric misses

I'm an Anti-Fraud Specialist at 4bill.io. I watch pay-in and payout transactions in real time – and dig through the history – to catch suspicious behaviour while it's still cheap to stop.

Most cases come down to cross-referencing several signals at once – IP, device fingerprints, BINs, geolocation, velocity and transaction amounts – in SQL (Athena, PostgreSQL) and Python, to catch what a single metric would miss.

The numbers I live by: TPR, FPR, precision, recall, fraud rate and loss rate – and the dashboards that put them in front of the whole team in real time. One view everyone trusts, fast enough to act on.

Before 4bill.io – and alongside it – I ran freelance analytics on product, email and sales data across 15+ markets, from raw SQL to the final read-out.
